For DivHacks 2020, I worked in a team of 4 people to build a mock up for an app designed to support Community Engagement Track Winner in DivHacks 2020 for designing a mutual aid app with React Native. On this team I created the low-fidelity design with Balsamiq, help translate that to Figma, then build the User Workflow and Business workflow

The app won first place for its track, Community engagement.
