In this 10 hour hackathon I worked in a team of 4 to design and build a prototype for a web-based, real-time feedback tool for classrooms, called Quest. The tool was designed so that students could submit questions from personal devices to the teacher screen to help alleviate social anxiety when asking questions in large lectures. I focused on building the backend using mostly Node.js. 

In the end, the project won first place.
