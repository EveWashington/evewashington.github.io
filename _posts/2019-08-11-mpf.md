As chairperson of this intern project at Blue Cross Blue Shield of Michigan (BCBSM), I led a team partnered with the Michigan Parkinson’s Foundation (MPF). We created a database of 900+ long-term care providers in Michigan for Parkinson's patients, and designed an outreach plan for MPF. At the end of the summer we presented our recommendations to hundreds of BCBSM employees and executives.
