I analyzed a database with over 60,000 job postings from LionShare/Handshake, then working with a visuals team produced and reported on trends in the job market and industry changes due to COVID-19 for an enterprise story. 

The story later went on to become a 2020 [SPJ Region-One Finalist](https://www.spj.org/news.asp?REF=1791) for Data Visualization, and a honorable mention in the 2020 [Pudding Cup](https://pudding.cool/process/pudding-cup-2020/)

See the story [here](https://www.columbiaspectator.com/eye-lead/2020/05/15/uncertainty-looms-as-student-jobs-are-disappearing-during-the-great-lockdown/)

See the analysis [here](https://github.com/graphicsdesk/covid-jobs)

**Fun Fact**: This story was my first time using R. I taught myself the language as I was doing the reporting, so the code is admittedly a little rougher than normal.
